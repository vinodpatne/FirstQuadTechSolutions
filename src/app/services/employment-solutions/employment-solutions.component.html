Employment solutions are services and strategies designed to help individuals find and secure employment, and to help employers find qualified candidates for job vacancies. Here are some common types of employment solutions:

  • Services that help individuals identify their skills and interests, explore career options and develop strategies for finding employment. This can include CV writing, networking and interview skills training.
  • Agencies that provide temporary employment opportunities, often leading to permanent placements. This can be a good option for jobseekers who are looking for the short-term work or for looking for temporary staff.
  • Services provided to employees who are made redundant or dismissed, including career transition assistance, job search assistance and counselling services.
  • Employment solutions play a crucial role in connecting job seekers with employers and helping individuals navigate the complexities of the job market. By providing support, resources and guidance, these solutions can help individuals find meaningful employment and contribute to a thriving workforce.
